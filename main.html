Paris, France


Paris, known as the "City of Light", is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world. It is famous for its art, culture, architecture, and world-class cuisine. The city’s charm lies in its historic monuments, cozy cafés, and breathtaking views of the Eiffel Tower.

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower – A symbol of Paris and one of the most iconic landmarks in the world.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa and thousands of other artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – Enjoy a romantic view of the city from the water.
  5. Montmartre – Known for its artistic streets and the stunning Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissants – Buttery and flaky, a true French breakfast classic.
  • Macarons – Sweet, colorful, and deliciously delicate.
  • Baguette – A crisp bread that is a staple in every Parisian meal.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory delights.
  • Escargots – A traditional French dish made of seasoned snails.
